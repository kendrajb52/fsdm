ABOUT US

The Lord gave Evangelist Windell McCowan a vision to start a church after several men and women of God spoke into his life. One of these men of God was Bishop John Baker of Harmony Baptist and another was McCowan's very own pastor Bishop Robert L Davis of Emmanuel's Upper Room COGIC in Reagor Springs, Texas.       Evangelist McCowan set out to do the work of the Lord. He found a store front located in Desoto, Texas at 1670 N Hampton Rd Ste 118-D. Bishop Davis was instrumental in helping us to transform the inside of the building into a beautiful sanctuary. On November 21, 1999, Free Spirit Deliverance Tabernacle with Pastor Windell, 1st Lady Avis McCowan, their family and a few others officially became a church installed by Bishop Robert Davis. The POWER of God really moved in the place. Many miracles took place and many souls came to Christ!!! Glory to God!!!       We have been through many trials and tribulations since, but God is faithful and has never forsaken us. Some that joined didn't stay for one reason or another, but God has added to the church recently. Praise the Lord!!! We are encouraged to stand together no matter what because "eyes hath not seen, nor ear heard, neither have entered into the heart of man, the things which God hath prepared for them that love Him"! Continue to pray for FSDM as we endeavor to complete the mission that God has ordered for us!!
